Introduction

My name is JOSEPH MUTYABA.
I'm a Ugandan, male and a resident in Kampala the capital of
Uganda. The earliest years of my life were spent in a Village in Lwengo district called Kabulasokke.
While there, i was groomed and raised up as a strong and cradle Catholic, with strict adherence to
the teaching of our Lord Jesus the Sovereign Christ.

It was in this very place that I received the three great sacramnts of Baptism, Penance and my first
"Holy Communion."
After this, at the age of ten, I was brought by mum to come and stay in Kampala.
In total I spent 9 years in Lwengo and the rest in different parts of Kampala.
